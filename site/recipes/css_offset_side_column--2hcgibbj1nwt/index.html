CSS Offset Sidebar Column

Details

  • Goal is a main column that's centered (or mostly centered) wish a side colum that's offset so the main column stays largely in the center

EXAMPLE

----
The paper box is full of thumb tacks. Lift the square stone over the fence.

The ramp led up to the wide highway. No cement will hold hard wood. Smoke poured out of every crack. He crawled with care along the ledge. This plank was made for walking on. Add salt before you fry the egg. The sofa cushion is red and of light weight. Pluck the bright rose without leaves. Split the log with a quick, sharp blow. The ink stain dried on the finished page. The colt reared and threw the tall rider.

The point of the steel pen was bent and twisted. No hardship seemed to keep him sad. The sun came up to light the eastern sky. The lake sparkled in the red hot sun. Jazz and swing fans like fast music. Bring your best compass to the third class. Torn scraps littered the stone floor.

Once we stood beside the shore. Tend the sheep while the dog wanders. Just hoist it up and take it away, He wrote his last novel there at the inn. The tree top waved in a graceful way. There are more than two factors here. Those words were the cue for the actor to leave.
